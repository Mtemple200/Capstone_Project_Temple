Capstone Project

About

The project is a tropical-themed 3D adventure platformer created in Unreal Engine 5.

How to Play

  • Left, Right, Up and Down to move with the keyboard controls
  • Jump to avoid traps
  • Collect coins around the island
  • Avoid spikes or you lose!

Story

You explore a mysterious tropical island full of traps and ancient treasure.
